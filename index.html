Recipe #1 - Grilled Cheese


Back
  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
White Bread 4 slices
Butter 3 tablespoons
Cheddar cheese 2 slices

Step 1: Preheat skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich.

Step 2: Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.

Step 3: Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter and slice of cheese.

Recipe #2 - Eggs Benedict


Back
  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
White wine vinegar 3 tablespoons
Large eggs 4
Toasting muffins 2
Hot Hollandaise sauce 1 batch
Parma ham 4 slices

Step 1: Bring a deep saucepan of water to the boil (at least 2 litres) and add 3 tbsp white wine vinegar. Break the eggs into 4 separate coffee cups or ramekins. Split the muffins, toast them and warm some plates.

Step 2: Swirl the vinegared water briskly to form a vortex and slide in an egg. It will curl round and set to a neat round shape. Cook for 2-3 mins, then remove with a slotted spoon.

Step 3: Repeat with the other eggs, one at a time, re-swirling the water as you slide in the eggs. Spread some sauce on each muffin, scrunch a slice of ham on top, then top with an egg. Spoon over the remaining hollandaise and serve at once.

Recipe #3 - Caramelized banana oatmeal


Back
  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Rolled Quaker oats 1 cup
Bananas 3
Coconut oil 1/4 cup
Real maple syrup 1/4 cup
Cinnamon Sprinkle
Vannila Dash
Other toppings To taste

Step 1: Cook the oatmeal according to package directions. For extra awesome creamy oatmeal, cook it in milk!

Step 2: Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the maple syrup, cinnamon, and vanilla, and let it bubble around for a little bit to get incorporated. It should be lightly foamy and bubbly.

Step 3: Add bananas and simmer for a few minutes on each side until soft and plump.

Step 4: Remove from heat. Stir half of bananas into oatmeal, and reserve the other half for topping. Add other desired toppings.
